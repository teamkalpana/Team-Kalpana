About Team Kalpana

Team Kalpana is a conglomerate of highly motivated and dedicated students. Learning

CanSat Competition offered us a chance to gain substantial experience, showcase skills, analyse and evaluate outcomes and uncover personal aptitude. It encouraged us to adopt innovative techniques and develop new ideas and skills. We got a very rare opportunity of experiencing an actual rocket launch and understand the design and launch specifications of a satellite. This competition excelled in providing such exposure and realistic outlook towards the competitive nature of real life in such a short span of time, which could not have happened in our average college lives.

CANSAT

The CanSat Competition is a prestigious design-build fly competition organized by The American Astronautical Society (AAS) and NASA that provides teams from across the globe with an opportunity to experience the design life-cycle of an aerospace system. It involves simulation of a real satellite, integrated within the volume and shape of a softdrink Can. The challenge is to fit all the major subsystems found in a satellite, such as power, sensors ans communication system, into this minimal volume .
